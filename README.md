# 💧🌿 Marvelous Dreamer 🌿💧

Welcome to **Marvelous Dreamer**, an app designed to turn your ideas into inspiring journeys of imagination and creativity. ✨  
Whether you're organizing your thoughts, visualizing your goals, or simply exploring new possibilities, Marvelous Dreamer helps you dream bigger and plan smarter.

---

## 📖 About the Project

**Marvelous Dreamer** was born from a simple idea: create an app that feels magical yet practical — something intuitive, elegant, and genuinely enjoyable to use.

This project was developed with a focus on **modern Android development practices**, ensuring a smooth, dynamic, and user-friendly experience. The goal was to build something I would personally love to use every day — clean design, seamless navigation, and meaningful features.

The app is built using **Kotlin + Jetpack Compose**, delivering a reactive and modern UI while maintaining strong performance and maintainability.

---

## 🔥 Key Features

✅ **Smart Planning System** – Organize your ideas and plans effortlessly. 📅  
✅ **Nearby Discovery** – Explore relevant places and inspiration around you. 🏝️  
✅ **Image Storage** – Save visual memories and creative references. 🖼️  
✅ **AI-Powered Recommendations** – Discover suggestions tailored to your interests. 🤖  
✅ **User Preference Customization ⚙️** – Personalize your experience.  
✅ **Multi-language Support 🌎** – Accessible to users worldwide.

---

## 📲 Technologies Used

- **Kotlin** – Primary programming language.
- **Jetpack Compose** – Modern declarative UI toolkit.
- **Navigation Component** – Smooth and structured screen management.
- **Material 3** – Clean, minimalist, and responsive design system.
- **Android Oreo (API 26+) Compatibility**  
  📌 **Reason**: Android Oreo provides a stable and widely adopted foundation, ensuring compatibility with most active Android devices while maintaining access to modern features.
